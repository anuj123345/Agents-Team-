How it works

From brief to deliverable in three steps

No prompting expertise needed. Write like you're briefing a human strategy team.

01
Write your brief

Describe the brand, campaign challenge, or marketing problem. Add context from files, URLs, or brand documents. The more context, the sharper the output.

02
Six agents get to work

Market research, audience analysis, brand strategy, content planning, copywriting, and campaign review run in sequence. Each agent builds on the last.

03
Deliver to clients

Switch to Client View for a clean presentation-ready layout. Export branded PDF reports with your agency name, client details, and date.

The team

Six specialists. One brief.

Each agent has a focused role and passes its findings to the next — building a complete strategy document layer by layer.

📊
Market Researcher
Maps trends, competitors & market opportunity
Competitive landscape, market sizing, growth trends, consumer behavior shifts, and emerging opportunities.
👥
Audience Analyst
Builds personas, segments & buying journey
Customer segments, psychographic profiles, buying triggers, channel preferences, and emotional jobs-to-be-done.
🎯
Brand Strategist
Develops positioning, messaging & differentiation
Brand positioning statement, messaging hierarchy, competitive whitespace, tone of voice, and strategic bets.
📝
Content Strategist
Plans content pillars, channels & 90-day calendar
Content pillars, channel mix rationale, funnel-stage content types, calendar structure, and KPI targets.
✍️
Copywriter
Writes headlines, taglines, CTAs & campaign copy
Brand tagline options, campaign headlines, CTAs by funnel stage, social captions, and email subject lines.
🔍
Campaign Critic
Stress-tests strategy & catches blind spots
Strategic risks, untested assumptions, competitive responses, messaging gaps, and specific fixes for each issue.
